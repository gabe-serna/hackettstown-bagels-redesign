Our Menu

Hackettstown Bagels and Deli offers the “best bagels around” with so many fresh varieties to choose from. More than just bagels, we also offer a great selection of deli sandwiches and homemade salads.

For those planning their next gathering, Hackettstown Bagel & Deli offers party bagels and catering. As a "thank you" to patrons, look for different coupons offered each month on bagels and catering.

Bagels Catering Employment

Best known for our bagels, which are made daily from homemade dough that is boiled and baked. Bagels can then be topped a number of ways for any-sized appetite, and for any early morning or midday craving.

About Us

Open 7 days a week, 365 days a year, we have the best bagels around. You can order online and pick up through our drive thru. If you bring your pet with you through the drive thru, we'll give them a special treat!

We invite you to come on down where you'll find fresh, hot bagels and mouthwatering flavors waiting for you. Experience the magic that has made Hackettstown Bagels and Deli a cherished destination for locals and visitors alike.
